## Design Elements

### Typography
- **Title**: Large, elegant serif or script font
- **Recipient Name**: Prominent, often with decorative underline
- **Body Text**: Readable serif, justified or centered
- **Credentials**: Bold, may use all caps

### Visual Elements
- **Border**: Ornate frame or simple elegant line
- **Seal**: Gold/silver embossed look or official stamp
- **Logos**: Issuing organization prominently displayed
- **Signatures**: Handwriting-style or scanned images
- **Badge**: Digital badge for sharing on LinkedIn

### Color Schemes

**Classic (Default)**
- Gold accents
- Navy or black text
- Cream background

**Modern**
- Brand colors
- Clean white background
- Minimal decoration

**Premium**
- Metallic gold/silver elements
- Deep blue or burgundy accents
- Textured background

## Output Formats

### Print (Default)
- 8.5" x 11" or A4
- High resolution for framing
- Includes bleed marks for professional printing

### Digital
- Shareable on social media
- LinkedIn badge format
- PDF with embedded verification link

### Wallet Card
- Credit card sized
- Essential info only
- QR code for full verification

## Print Optimization

- Premium paper (minimum 24lb)
- Full color printing recommended
- Consider gold foil stamping for seals
- UV coating for longevity
