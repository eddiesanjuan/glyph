## Typography and Formatting

- **Title**: Centered, uppercase, bold
- **Party Names**: Bold, uppercase in body
- **Section Titles**: Uppercase, bold, numbered
- **Subsection Numbers**: Indented with decimal notation
- **Legal Terms**: Initial caps when defined ("Agreement", "Services")
- **Body Text**: Justified alignment, 1.5 line spacing

## Page Layout

- **Margins**: 1.25" left and right (for binding)
- **Headers**: Agreement number and page number
- **Footers**: "Initials: _____ / _____" for multi-page acknowledgment
- **Section Breaks**: New page for signature section and exhibits

## Print Optimization

- 8.5" x 11" (Letter) or A4
- Clean page breaks between major sections
- Section numbers align with page top when possible
- Footer initials lines for legal validity
